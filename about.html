About Me

Hi, I’m Puja Maheshwari, a girl born and raised in the Bay Area, California, who somehow ended up in the middle of Indiana. This Fall I will be a junior at Purdue University majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Statistics.

Since I was young I have loved to dance, and have learned a variety of Indian dance types. The past couple years I’ve even been on a nationally competitive Indian dance team, Boiler Bhangra, at my school!

I also enjoy traveling and taking pictures. I believe traveling and experiencing a variety of cultures and backgrounds is extremely important. I hope my photography can capture how I see different cultures and countries through my eyes and perspective.

As a girl coder, I strongly believe in having an equal playing field in tech fields and hope to help work towards bridging the gender gap in STEM fields and spread more awareness of this issue. I aspire to one day start my own company and encourage other young girls to do the same.

I hope my website can serve as a platform to share my dancing, pictures, travel adventures, technical projects, and my other hobbies include cooking, discovering new viewpoints, and sipping coffee!!
