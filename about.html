About Me

Hi, I’m Puja Maheshwari! I am currently a junior at Purdue University majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Statistics. I was born and raised in the Bay Area, California.

Since I was young I have loved to dance and have learned a variety of Indian dance types. Over the past couple years I’ve been on a nationally competitive Indian dance team, Boiler Bhangra, at my school!

In my free time I enjoy traveling and taking pictures. I believe traveling and experiencing a variety of cultures and backgrounds is extremely important to opening our eyes and bringing us closer together. Through my photography I hope to capture the small similarities in the diversity of different cultures and countries.

As a girl coder, I strongly believe in having an equal playing field in tech. I want to take part in the movement of bridging the gender gap in STEM fields especially by spreading more awareness of this issue.

I hope my website can serve as a platform to share not only my dancing, pictures, travel adventures, and technical projects, but also my other hobbies such as cooking, discovering new viewpoints, and sipping coffee!!

If you would like to contact me for a technical or photography collaboration, please email me at flytheunknown@gmail.com. You can also reach me at any of my social media accounts linked below!
